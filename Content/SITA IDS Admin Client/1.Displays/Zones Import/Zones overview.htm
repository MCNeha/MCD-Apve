Zones Overview

The View Zones window displays the groupings of a Parent Zone and associated Child Zones and Child Devices.

You can create and manage specific zones (locations) so that information can be displayed on specific devices within those specific zones/locations.

Note: Depending on your administration permissions, you may or may not have the ability to create Zones.

To Search for a Zone or Device, input your details into the Search text field to find your required Zone or Device.


In the View Zones window you can also:

  • Add a Zone

  • Edit a Zone

  • Delete a Zone
