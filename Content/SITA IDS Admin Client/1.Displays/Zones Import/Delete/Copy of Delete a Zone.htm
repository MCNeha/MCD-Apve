Delete a Zone

  1. Open any of the saved zone.

  2. If the zone was saved with the protected status enabled, then you'll notice that the delete zone is automatically greyed out and is in protected state.

  1. Go up and uncheck the protected status. You will be given the opportunity to delete it.

  1. Click on the delete zone button.

  2. You will get a confirmation box.

  1. If you delete any sub zone which in turn affects other zones. Then it will reflect a message saying which other zones are affected by deleting that sub zone.
