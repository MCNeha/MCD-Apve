Edit a Zone

Note: Depending on your administration permissions, you may or may not have the ability to create Zones.

  1. Click on the Zone you want to edit

  2. The Edit Zonepanel will display

  3. Note: the Zone Management Hierarchy menu on the left hand side of the window will list your selected zone and other zones.

  1. Note: the Delete Zone button is only available in the Edit Zone window.

  2. Check the Protected Status toggle if required and not already checked. Your selection will disenable the Delete Zone button .

  1. If youSelect or Clear a Zone, the Zone Hierarchy will be updated automatically.
