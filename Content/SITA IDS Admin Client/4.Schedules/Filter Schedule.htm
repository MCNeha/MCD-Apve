Filter Schedule

You can Filter Schedules by selecting the Airline from the drop-down list.

Filter Schedule Action

Arrival

Filter Schedule by Airline based on Arrival.
Departure

Filter Schedule by Airline based on Departure.
Effective

Filter Schedule by Airline based on Effective airlines.

Note: By Default, the schedules are displayed based on the effective airlines.
Expired

Filter Schedule by Airline based on Expired.
