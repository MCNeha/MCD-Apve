Check-in Design

The Check In design options allows you to customize the design and display of media and announcements of your check-in counter.

Open/Close a Check-in Location

  1. Select a Check-in counter location

  1. Click the Check-in location toggle button to open or close the check-in counter.
    The check-in counter will display OPEN or CLOSED depending on your choice.

Add/Edit an Airline Logo

  1. Click Airline from the Check-in design section.
  2. In the select an airline logo field, click Browse to upload a new logo file from your local file manager.
  3. Click Publish.
    The updated logo is saved and published.
  4. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Add/Edit a Service Type

  1. Click Service from the Check-in design section.
  2. Select a Service Type from the available options.
  3. Click Publish.
    The updated service type is saved and published.
  4. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Add an Announcement

  1. Click Announcements from the Check-in design section.
  2. Select an Announcement Type from the drop-down.
  3. Enter a Message.
  4. Click Add.
  5. The announcement is added to the queue to display on the check-in screen.

Note: Maximum four text message (pre-defined or custom) can be added.

A warning message will display if you try to add a fifth message.

  1. Edit or Remove any Announcements queued, if necessary.
  2. Click Publish.
    The new announcement is saved and published.
  3. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Edit an Announcement

  1. Click Announcements from the Check-in design section.
  2. Click Edit next to the announcement in the Announcements queued section.
    The Edit Announcement window displays.
  3. Edit the text message.
  4. Click Save.
  5. Click Publish.
    The edited announcement is saved and published.
  6. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Remove an Announcement

  1. Click the Announcements from the Check-in design section.
  2. Click Remove next to the announcement in the Announcements queued section.
    The announcement is removed.
  3. Click Publish.
    The changes made to the announcements for the check-in screen are saved and published. 
  4.  Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Display Eligible Flights

  1. Click Flights from the Check-in design section.
  2. Click SHOW or HIDE to display or hide the currently eligible flights for check in at the counter.
  3. Click Publish.
    The selected display flights setting is saved and published.
  4. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Update the Check-in Counter Style

  1. Click Style from the Check-in design section.
  2. Select the Colors for display:
    1. Select the Background color.
    2. Select the Text color.
  3. Select the Background image:
    1. Click the Change link to select the Background image.
      The Select content window displays.
    2. Select tag name.(This is an Optional field)
    3. Click Add Filter to apply the filter.
    4. Select a check-in logo.
    5. Click SELECT.
  4. Click Publish.
    The updated check-in counter style is saved and published.
  5. Click Revert to reset back all check-in design settings to their last publication.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q 1 - How can I select/assign multiple check-in counters for the same flight while adding new flight?

This feature is not currently available in the client, but will be available in the future.

 Q 2 - How can I select a current flight for a check-in counter?

Check-in counters will only display flights that are assigned to that check-in.

 Q 3 - How can I verify a check-in counter design?

Check-in templates can be verified in a web browser.

  1. Click the Check-in name.
    • The Select Check-in window displays.
  2. Click the necessary check-in location name.
  3. Click the Style menu in the Check-in design section.
