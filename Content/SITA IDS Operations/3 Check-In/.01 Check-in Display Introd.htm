Check in

A Check in counter screen displays media and announcements associated with a flight. You can configure the display of media and announcements using a number of design options.

Design Options

The Check In design options allows you to customize the design and display of media and announcements of your check-in counter.

The range of items to edit and display on a check in counter include an Airline Logo,a Service Type, an Announcement, Eligible Flights and Style.

Open or Close a Check in Counter

  1. Select a Check-in counter location

  1. Click the Check-in location toggle button to open or close the check-in counter.
    The check-in counter will display OPEN or CLOSED depending on your choice.
