Replay Boarding Sequence

  1. Click the gate number or the icon next to it.
  2. Choose a departure gate number from the list.
    • All departure flights assigned to the selected gate are displayed under the selected departure gate number.
  3. Select a flight.
    • The boarding sequence displays in the Boarding Sequences panel.
  4. Click Next on a boarding sequence step to cycle through the step.
  5. Click Replay to restart a boarding sequence step.
