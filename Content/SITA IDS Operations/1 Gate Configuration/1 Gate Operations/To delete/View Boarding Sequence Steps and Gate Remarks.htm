Sequence Steps and Gate Remarks

To view the boarding sequence steps and gate remarks

  1. Click the GATE module in the sidebar menu on the left of the screen.
    • The Gate Operations screen displays.
  2. Click the gate number or the icon next to it.
  3. Choose a departure gate number from the list.
    • All departure flights assigned to the selected gate are displayed under the selected departure gate number.
  4. Select a flight.
    • The boarding sequence displays on the right of the screen.
