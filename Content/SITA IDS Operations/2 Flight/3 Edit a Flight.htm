Edit a Flight

  1. Click on the Flight you want to edit.
    The Edit Flight dialog box will display.

  2. Amend the required flight details.

    Note: You can associate a Toast Message with the flight.

  3. Click Save to save the flight details.

     

  4. Optional: Click Cancel to clear the flight details and exit the form.

Note: Grayed out fields such as Flight Type, Carrier Name, Flight Number, Scheduled Time and City are not configurable.
Note: You can associate a Toast Message with the flight.
