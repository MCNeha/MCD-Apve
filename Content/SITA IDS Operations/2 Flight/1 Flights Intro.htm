Flights

The FLIGHT module also referred to asthe flight tracking grid, displays all the flights in a standard flight card layout from the daily schedule

Key Flight Filter Options

You can view flight details using three top level filter options:

All Flights, Arrivals and Departures.

Additional Filters

Five additional filters: Airlines, Cities,Checkins, Claims, Gates can also be used to filter flight details.

Search Flight

To Search for a flight input your details into the Search text field to find the required flight.
